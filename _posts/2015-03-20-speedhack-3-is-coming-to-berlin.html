After two successful editions in Chicago and in Paris, we are bringing SpeedHack to APIDays+APIStrat Berlin.

SpeedHack is a new franchise of hackathons of a new genre as we wrote in an earlier post here. Hackers have only 3 hours to solve code challenges suggested by our API partners. The team that solves all the challenges -- plus some extra challenges -- wins the global prize!

SpeedHack events are a great opportunity for hackers to get exposed to different sets of APIs, that they may use in their projects. API partners also take advantage of SpeedHack by meeting the local community of hackers and getting exposure and feedback. It's a great way to see how the API is perceived by the community.

Participation in as a hacker in the SpeedHack is free.
