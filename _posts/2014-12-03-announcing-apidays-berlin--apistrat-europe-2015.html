We're excited to announce our next API Strategy & Practice Event for which we're joining forces with APIdays! Yes, that's right. APIDays Berlin & APIStrat Europe 2015 will take place in Berlin next April, 24th and 25th of 2015.

The joint conference will feature leading keynotes, panels and in-depth sessions on both general API strategies and practical knowledge across a wide range of industry sectors. The event will also include workshops and a Speedhack code competition, as well as local support from SPHERE.IO, local organizer of the recent APIDays Berlin 2014.

Our speaker lineup includes Mandy Waite, Developer Advocate at Google Cloud Platform; Chris Taggart, CEO of OpenCorporates; and Max Harlow from the Guardian Open Platform.

As always, we want to say thank you to our kind sponsors and supporters:

If your company is interested in supporting the event by sponsoring, please visit our website, or contact us for more details.

If you're interested in submitting a talk for consideration a call for talks is now open. The deadline for submissions is the 23rd of January. Tickets for the event will be on sale in early January and initial speakers and sponsors announced over the coming weeks.
