Thank you for registering for APIStrat 2016 in Boston - we're looking forward to seeing you at the event!


HOTEL

If you'll be visiting Boston for the event, discounted room rates are available at the Marriott Long Wharf. Please use this link to book in order to take advantage of the special APIStrat rate: http://bit.ly/APIStratBostonHotel

ADMISSION

Please bring your tickets to the event entrance (either printed or show us on your phone).

TICKET TRANSFERS

You are able to transfer your ticket to another name if you ask for a transfer up to 48 hours prior to the event. Just send us an email with the following information: order number, your name, and the name and email address of the person you want to transfer the ticket to.

REFUNDS

We do accept ticket refunds. The last day to accept ticket refunds is September 1st, 2016. We will not be able to issue any ticket refunds after September 1st. Thank you!
